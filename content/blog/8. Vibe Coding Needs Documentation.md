Vibe Coding is only as good as your documentation.

Here is my 3-Tool Framework.

"Vibe coding" doesn't mean "no planning." In fact, the better the plan, the better the vibe. I've refined a workflow that bridges the gap between a high-level idea and a production-ready codebase using ChatGPT, Grok, and Cursor.

The Workflow:

- The Foundation (ChatGPT): I start by generating a Technical Spec/PRD. ChatGPT remains the gold standard for structured document creation, defining the What and the Why.
- The Logic Engine (Grok): I move the PRD into a Grok Project. Grok excels at the back-and-forth required to map out complex business logic. We iterate until we have 1-3 Business Logic Markdown files that define every user flow.
- The Prompt Engineering: Before touching code, I have Grok generate specific Bootstrap and Implementation Prompts based on those logic files.
- The Execution (Cursor Pro): I organize everything into a /docs folder (Business Logic + Prompts). I feed these into Cursor's Planning Agent. For every path, I ask Cursor: "Create a plan for Prompt X using Logic Y."
- The Build: Once the plans are saved to the workspace, I hit Build step-by-step.

The Result: A "dry," efficient, and highly predictable development cycle. You aren't just coding; you're orchestrating.
