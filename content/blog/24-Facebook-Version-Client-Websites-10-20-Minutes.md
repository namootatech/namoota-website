## How I build great-looking client websites in 10-20 minutes (and what I use)

A lot of you have asked how I ship client sites so fast. Here's the exact workflow - no gatekeeping.

I need 3 things from the client: business name, industry (e.g. restaurant, salon, gym), and a short description. If they don't send a description, I use whatever they did send or I generate one in under a minute with Gemini or Grok - just paste name + industry and ask for a 2-3 sentence "About" for the site.

Then:

Step 1 - I use the Vercel v0 Website Prompt Generator on Agent.ai. I feed it the name, industry and description. It gives me a ready-made prompt that is tuned for v0 so I don't have to guess the wording.

Step 2 - I open v0.dev, new chat, paste that prompt + the description, and hit Generate. In 5 minutes or less I have a full landing site (hero, sections, styling). I might ask for one or two small tweaks in the same chat (e.g. "make the CTA button blue" or "add a testimonials section"), then I'm done.

Step 3 - I add the client's custom domain and deploy on Vercel. Once DNS is pointed, the site is live.

That's it. Total time from "here's my business name and industry" to live site: 10-20 minutes. I've used this for restaurants, salons, small practices, NPOs and local businesses. It's my go-to for marketing and simple client sites - not for heavy e-commerce or super complex apps, but for most "I need a proper website" briefs it works a charm.

If you try it, drop a comment and tell me what you would add or change. Always keen to hear how others are doing it.
